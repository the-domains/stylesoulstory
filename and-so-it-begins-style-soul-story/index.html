And so it begins - style, soul, story

It was in my home economics class at school in Penang, Malaysia, that I discovered I loved making clothes. The first piece I made was a brightly coloured waistcoat, vibrantly patterned with a hideous, early 90s mix of reds and fluorescent yellows and blues and greens on the front and black on the back.
